## Summary
A minimal, privacy-focused X (Twitter) client for posting and self-threading.

## Description
x-post-mobile is a lightweight, open-source mobile client for X (formerly Twitter) designed for simplicity and privacy. It focuses on the core experience of posting tweets and creating threads by replying to yourself.

**Features:**
*   **Post Tweets:** Send text and image tweets directly to X.
*   **Thread Support:** Easily create threads by replying to your own tweets with a long-press action.
*   **Privacy Focused:** Your API keys are stored locally on your device.
*   **Open Source:** Fully open source and transparent.
